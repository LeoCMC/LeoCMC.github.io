Keelung City
Food
In Keelung, food is an emotional symphony, each dish a vibrant story of heritage and flavor. From sizzling street delights to fresh seafood, every bite is a heartfelt embrace of Taiwanese culinary passion.
Attractions
Keelung beckons with its majestic harbor views, serene temples steeped in tradition, and the bustling Miaokou Night Market's sensory feast. Each attraction resonates with history, spirituality, and the vibrant essence of this Taiwanese gem.
History
Keelung's history is a saga of perseverance, etched by seafaring legacies, foreign influences, and the unwavering spirit of its inhabitants. It's a narrative of resilience, woven into the fabric of this coastal haven's soul.
